We are

The Green Place

Established in 2010, The Green Place emerged as a visionary enterprise with humble beginnings, initially based from a small apartment in Harrisburg, PA. Our founders played pivotal roles in shaping the landscape of sustainable business practices, drawing from their expertise in environmental science and sustainability. Our journey began with a commitment to creating a positive impact on the planet, and since then, we have evolved into a leading force in the sustainable products and environmental engineering sector. As we witnessed the global shift towards sustainable practices, The Green Place adapted and thrived, aligning our mission with the urgent need for eco-conscious solutions.

From the development of eco-friendly consumer products to revolutionary environmental engineering projects, we are dedicated to providing comprehensive and impactful solutions that contribute to a greener and more sustainable world.
