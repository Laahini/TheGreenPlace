Our Story

We are

The Green Place

Established in 2010, The Green Place emerged as a visionary enterprise with humble beginnings, initially based from a small apartment in Denver, Colorado. Our journey began with a commitment to creating a positive impact on the planet, and since then, we have evolved into a leading force in the sustainable products and environmental engineering sector. As we witnessed the global shift towards sustainable practices, The Green Place adapted and thrived, aligning our mission with the urgent need for eco-conscious solutions.

From the development of eco-friendly consumer products to revolutionary environmental engineering projects, we are dedicated to providing comprehensive and impactful solutions that contribute to a greener and more sustainable world.

The Green Place

Our

Name

The name "The Green Place" originated from a collective vision of cultivating a business that not only thrives economically but also blossoms ethically and sustainably. It symbolizes a commitment to environmental consciousness and a dedication to creating a space where green innovations flourish. As we embarked on this journey, "The Green Place" became a symbol of our mission to provide eco-friendly solutions, making it more than just a company name – it's a pledge to nurture a greener, more sustainable world for generations to come.
